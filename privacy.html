Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Application: Content Pipeline

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Content Pipeline ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our social media content management service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens provided by social media platforms (TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube) when you authorize the App to manage your accounts.
  • Content data: Text, media files, captions, and scheduling information for posts you create and publish through the App.
  • Platform identifiers: Your social media account IDs and page IDs necessary for publishing content.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Publishing and scheduling content on your authorized social media accounts
  • Managing your content queue and publishing history
  • Maintaining authentication with connected social media platforms

4. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens and content data are stored securely using encrypted storage services. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Access tokens are stored in environment variables and encrypted databases, not in source code.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party platforms through their official APIs:

  • TikTok (Content Posting API)
  • Facebook / Meta (Graph API)
  • Instagram (Graph API)
  • YouTube (Data API)

When you authorize the App with these platforms, your data is also subject to their respective privacy policies. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to publish content on your behalf.

6. TikTok Data Usage

When you connect your TikTok account, the App:

  • Requests permission to publish videos on your behalf (video.publish)
  • Stores your TikTok access token and refresh token securely for ongoing access
  • Does not access your TikTok messages, followers list, or personal profile data beyond what is necessary for publishing
  • Does not sell, share, or transfer your TikTok data to any third parties

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or content data with third parties, except:

  • When required to publish content to your authorized social media platforms
  • When required by law or legal process

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. When you revoke the App's access through a platform's settings, we will delete the associated tokens.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the App's access to your social media accounts at any time through each platform's settings
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, please contact us at: content.pipeline.app@gmail.com
